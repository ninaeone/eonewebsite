Our Founding

A House Built Different

Maison was founded in 2010 by a pair of collectors — one a veteran of Dubai's real estate market, the other a lifelong horologist — who recognized that ultra-high-net-worth clients deserved something the market simply didn't offer: a single, trusted partner for their most significant asset acquisitions.

We are not a portal. We are not a broker in the conventional sense. We are advisors who happen to operate one of the most active private transaction networks in the GCC.
